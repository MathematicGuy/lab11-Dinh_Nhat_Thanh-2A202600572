HITL Threshold Canvas

Use this to design one escalation rule at a time in the Day-11 banking agent.

Field What to write
Decision point The exact step where the assistant must decide: allow, refuse, or escalate
Failure harm What goes wrong if the model auto-decides incorrectly?
Trigger signal Amount threshold, confidence score, anomaly flag, auth mismatch, policy score, repeated attack attempts
Default action Allow, refuse, or ask clarifying questions before escalation
Escalation owner Fraud analyst, support agent, compliance reviewer, or supervisor
Latency budget How long can this user wait before trust drops or business impact rises?
Rule: every HITL threshold should point to a specific harm. If you cannot name the harm, the threshold is probably arbitrary.
